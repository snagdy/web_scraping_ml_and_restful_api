The majority of properties sold in London over the past year were flat properties. The average price they sold for was £606,093. Terraced properties had an average sold price of £916,428 and semi-detached properties had an average sold price of £1,008,389.

London, with an overall average price of £752,881, was found to be more expensive than nearby Marylebone (£655,625), but cheaper than Holborn (£2,095,375) and Kennington (£925,000).

In the past year sold property prices in London were 8% higher when compared to the year before and 13% higher when compared to 2015 when average prices were £658,189.
